Healthcare

Infusion pumps are used to administer medication over a period of time, with both syringe drivers and peristaltic pumps being widely used for chemotherapy, pain management and end-of-life care. Insulin pumps for diabetics are small, battery-operated infusion pumps.

Scientific research

Syringe pumps are used widely in scientific labs, especially in the chemical and biomedical environments. They are used for everything from fluid-reward systems for research animals to bioprinting and microfluidics.

Other uses

Syringe pumps are used in 3D food printers, from printing chocolate to molecular gastronomy. They also have uses in manufacturing, are used in aquariums for medicating fish and in underwater gliders for adjusting buoyancy.
